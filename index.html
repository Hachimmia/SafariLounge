Welcome to

Menu Book

The Safari Lounge opened in 2013 and since then we have been committed every day to offering great and friendly service accompanied by super imaginative food that comes from all over the world.
Our idea is a fusion cuisine that unites all cultures in something pleasant and good for your palate. All this in a very special place that you will not find very easily around. Our place is open for everybody: we have a large choice of vegetarian food and gluten free, people that looking for some special plates and dogs are welcomed as well.
Situated in the tropical surroundings of Abbeyhill, Edinburgh, all The Safari Lounge staff are ready to welcome you.
What a magical place to have lunch/dinner! :D
